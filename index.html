Kpop Dance Group

About

XTRM is a dance group at Stanford that specializes in Kpop (Korean Pop) and other forms of East-Asian popular dance (JPop, Chinese pop, etc).

We perform dance covers of existing pieces from Kpop groups like EXO or Girls Generation. We also invent and perform our own dance choreography to Kpop songs.

We welcome members from all backgrounds who are interested in dance and East-Asian pop culture.

The name of the group is pronounced "extreme". It has no meaning in particular :P
